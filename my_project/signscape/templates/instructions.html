How to Play

Welcome to the SignScape Game!

Instructions:

  1. An image of a sign will be displayed on the screen, representing a specific word or concept in sign language.
  2. Below the image, four multiple-choice options will be provided.
  3. Your task is to select the correct meaning of the sign from the given options.
  4. Choose the option that best matches the sign you see, and see if you can correctly identify the meaning!
  5. Test your knowledge and see how many signs you can accurately interpret within the time limit.
